Well-being will remind you to take a rest while working and that you can always continue to the work after the break.

Inspired by https://github.com/alexanderepstein/Sandman a website that calculates optimal times to fall asleep based on when you want to wake up.

It will notify you throughout your work to take breaks for water, and for better eye care
